# ChemistryExpanded  

**Version:** 1.3.0  
**Author:** Milky-way Galaxy  
**Description:** Expand your chemistry horizons! This mod introduces water electrolysis and paves the way for more chemistry-focused features in the future.  

---

## Why I Made This Mod  

I created ChemistryExpanded to add more depth to the game through chemistry. Starting with water electrolysis, I aim to include a variety of chemical processes and reactions, enriching the gameplay with more complex and realistic systems.  

---

## Features  

- **Electrolysis of Water:** Convert water into hydrogen through a new recipe.  
- **Foundation for More Chemistry:** This is just the beginning—future updates will include additional chemical processes and systems.  

---

## Installation  

1. Make sure you have the required dependencies installed:  
   - [BepInEx 5.4.17](https://github.com/xiaoye97/BepInEx)  
   - [LDBTool 2.0.6](https://github.com/xiaoye97/LDBTool)  
   - [CommonAPI 1.6.3](https://github.com/CommonAPI)  
2. Download `ChemistryExpanded.dll` from the [Releases](https://github.com/Milky-way-Galaxy/ChemistryExpanded/releases).  
3. Place the `.dll` file into your `BepInEx/plugins` directory.
